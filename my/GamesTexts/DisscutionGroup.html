Discussion group is a lively inter-office social arena which allows you to share any type of information - such as presentations, videos, new company protocols etc. – with a predetermined group of employees and receive their feedback. In addition to providing a convenient instructional tool, the Discussion Group acts as a social arena where employees can interact and voice their opinions in a productive and constructive manner. The discussion group is a highly effective means of creating employee’ involvement in the subject of your choice and improve performance by exchanging views and ideas among each other.

Game Objective:

To give feedback and share opinions regarding a specific link/topic/question posed. ----------------------------------------------------------------------

Benefits:

  • Learn and share new information.
  • Boost morale & motivation.
  • Receive direct employee feedback.
  • Improve collaboration among employees.
----------------------------------------------------------------------

Game Description:

Participants are invited to join a discussion group where they are asked to give their feedback and/or thoughts regarding specific topics raised by the group’s manager.
